Looming Showdown Between Azimio and UDA

Showdown between Azimio and Kenya Kwanza MPs is expected in parliament this afternoon when the 2023 proposed finance fill will be tabled before the house. The lawmakers will vote at 2PM

Raila's Latest Statement Sends Shockwaves in State House as He Reveals Next Move

opposition chief Raila Odinga has today declared that nationwide demos will kick off on Monday 20th

He has called upon his supporters to turn out in large numbers

The former Prime Minister is demanding the government to publicly open the IEBC servers. He also wants the government to address the issue of the high cost of living and scrap off the 2023 finance bill

Govt Sends Stern Warning Ahead of Planned Maandamano

The government through the Inspector General of Police Japtheth Koome has today warned Kenyans against engaging themselves in demonstrations.

Speaking at the National Police headquarters here in Nairobi, Koome declared the planned protests by Azimio illegal. He said that those who will be cought will face the law

Koome further said that those in opposition should use legal means to channel their grievances and not through destructive protests
